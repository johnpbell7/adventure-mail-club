Zara's story

The girl the animals trust.

Zara has always understood animals better than people. Not in a magical way — she just pays attention. She notices when a bird changes its song, when a fox turns left instead of right, when the forest goes quiet for no reason.

She's the group's naturalist. She carries a field journal, pressed flowers, and a panda toy named Bao that she's had since she was three. Animals trust her instantly — which is useful when you need to cross a river guarded by wolves.

Her quest is to free the Caged Firebird — a legendary creature trapped deep in the Thornwood. Each chapter brings her closer, but the cage can only be opened by someone the forest trusts completely.
