Help centre

Answers to the most common questions.

Getting started

How do I subscribe?

Choose a plan on our pricing page, fill in your child's details, and complete payment. Your first letter will arrive within 5 working days.

Can I subscribe as a gift?

Yes. Visit our gift page and choose a 3, 6, or 12-month gift subscription. We will send you a gift voucher to give on the day.

What age is it for?

Adventure Mail Club is designed for children aged 5 to 10. Younger children will need help reading; older children engage independently.

Delivery

When will my first letter arrive?

We dispatch within 5 working days of your subscription starting. Royal Mail First Class delivery typically takes 1 to 2 days.

Do you ship outside the UK?

Not currently. We ship to all UK mainland addresses including Northern Ireland, and most Scottish islands.

My letter hasn't arrived

Allow up to 10 working days from dispatch before contacting us. If it still hasn't arrived, we will resend immediately.

Subscriptions & billing

How do I cancel?

Go to My Account, then Subscription, and click Cancel. Your subscription ends at the end of the current billing period.

Can I pause my subscription?

Yes, you can pause for up to 3 months from your account dashboard. Your billing pauses and resumes when you unpause.

How do I update my billing details?

Go to My Account, then Payment Methods. You can add or replace a card at any time.

The portal

How do I access the portal?

Each letter contains a QR code. Scan it to access the portal on any device. You can also log in at adventuremailclub.co.uk/portal.

What is in the portal?

The portal includes a world map, your child's inventory of items collected, a travel log, character profiles, and mission missions that complement each letter.

My QR code isn't working

Try scanning in good light, or go directly to portal.html and enter your login details manually.

Can't find your answer?

We aim to respond to all queries within one working day.
