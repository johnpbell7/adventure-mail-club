For schools & educators

Bring the adventure into class.

Adventure Mail Club works in classroom settings as a creative writing stimulus, a reading comprehension vehicle, and a cross-curriculum literacy project. Children receive personalised letters as a class group or individually.

Curriculum-aligned literacy by post

We work with Key Stage 1 and Key Stage 2 classes across the UK. Curriculum resources, reading guides and teacher notes are included with all school subscriptions.
