Returns & refunds

Last updated: 1 January 2026

Cancellation policy

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You will not be charged again after cancellation, and you will continue to receive letters until the period ends.

Refunds

We do not offer refunds for subscription payments already processed. If you cancel partway through a month, you will retain access to that month's content. Exceptions may be made at our discretion in cases of technical failure on our part.

Damaged or lost letters

If your letter arrives damaged or does not arrive at all, please contact us within 14 days of the expected delivery date. We will resend the letter free of charge. We do not offer refunds for delivery issues — we will always resend.

Incorrect personalisation

If your letter contains errors due to a mistake on our part (not information you provided incorrectly), we will resend a corrected letter free of charge. Please contact us within 14 days of receipt.

Gift subscriptions

Gift subscriptions are non-refundable once the first letter has been dispatched. If the gift has not yet been dispatched, contact us and we will cancel and refund in full.

How to contact us

To request a resend or raise a returns query, email hello@adventuremailclub.co.uk with your order number and a brief description. We aim to respond within 1 working day.
