FAQ

Common questions.

If the answer you need isn't here, drop us a line and we'll get back to you the same day.

Can I cancel at any time?
Yes. Cancel any time before your next billing date with no penalty. Your child's portal access continues until the end of the paid period.
How soon does the first letter arrive?
The first letter is dispatched within 5 working days of subscribing. Subsequent letters arrive in the first week of each month.
What's the difference between Explorer and Premium?
Both tiers include the full personalised adventure letter, portal access and write-back feature. Premium adds extra adventure items, bonus puzzles, occasional surprise gifts and priority reply processing.
What if my child doesn't write back?
The story continues regardless — but it takes a slightly different direction when no reply is received. Replies genuinely enrich the experience, but the letter always arrives either way.
Can I subscribe for multiple children?
Yes. Multi-child discounts apply from the second subscription. Each child gets their own fully personalised story, letters, and portal account.
What countries do you ship to?
Currently UK only for postal deliveries. International digital-only access is coming soon. Join the waitlist at the bottom of our homepage.
