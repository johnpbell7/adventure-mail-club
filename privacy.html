Privacy policy

Last updated: 1 January 2026

Who we are

Adventure Mail Club Ltd is a UK-registered company. We operate the website adventuremailclub.co.uk and the related subscription service. Our registered address is available on request. For privacy queries, contact us at privacy@adventuremailclub.co.uk.

What data we collect

We collect: your name and email address when you create an account; your child's first name, age, and interests to personalise the letters; your delivery address; payment information processed securely by Stripe (we do not store card numbers); and usage data from our website via cookies (see our Cookie Policy).

How we use your data

We use your data to: deliver your subscription and personalise each letter; send transactional emails (order confirmations, shipping updates); send our newsletter if you opt in; and improve our service. We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your child's data for any purpose other than personalising their letters.

Data retention

We keep your account data for as long as your subscription is active, plus 3 years after cancellation for legal and financial purposes. You can request deletion at any time by emailing us.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to: access the data we hold about you; correct inaccurate data; request deletion; restrict or object to processing; and data portability. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address above. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the ICO (ico.org.uk).

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services: Stripe for payment processing; Mailchimp for email delivery; Google Analytics for anonymised website analytics; and Supabase for our portal database. Each service has its own privacy policy. We have Data Processing Agreements in place with all processors.

Children's privacy

Our service involves data about children. We collect only a first name, age, and interests — the minimum needed to personalise letters. We never contact children directly. All communications are with the parent or guardian account holder. Children's data is never used for marketing or analytics.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you by email of any material changes. Continued use of the service after notification constitutes acceptance.
