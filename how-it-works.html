How the adventure works.

Pick a character. Get the first letter. Scan the code, make a choice, and watch the map start moving.

Step one

Pick a character & personalise

Choose from the Wandering Five. Add your child's name, city, age, and interests. The adventure is built around them — every letter references their world.

Step two

A letter lands on the mat

Every month, a personalised letter arrives through the letterbox. Inside: clues, a physical artefact to find, and a QR code that unlocks the portal. It's real post, not an app.

Step three

Scan. Choose. Shape the story.

Scan the QR code to log the letter, add artefacts to their inventory, and pick from 3–5 choices that decide what happens next. Different children get different stories.

Step four

Watch the map move

Every choice moves the map. The inventory grows. The story edges closer to its goal — and your child decides how they get there. 12 chapters. One unforgettable adventure.
