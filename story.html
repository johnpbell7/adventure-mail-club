The story so far.

A 12-month mystery. One sixth member. Your child.

The world

The Vanishing Atlas — a 12-chapter mystery.

Three years ago, five young explorers found a box buried beneath the oldest oak in Whispering Hollow Wood. The name on the lid — in letters of hammered gold — was not any of theirs.

The box contained a blank map and a single note: “The Sixth Member will know what this means. Find them before the first frost.”

The Wandering Five have been searching ever since — hidden libraries, canal worlds, buried archives. Every location points to one person. The sixth member.

Your child. And now they have found them.
