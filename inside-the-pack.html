What arrives every month.

Every pack is designed to feel like a real artefact from inside the story — not a product.

The letter

400–600 words
written for them.

The adventure letter is the heart of each pack. It is written directly to your child — their name, their city, and the detail from their last reply all appear naturally in the text, not bolted on.

Each letter advances the ongoing 12-chapter mystery. The Wandering Five are somewhere specific, something has happened, and your child is needed to help work out what comes next.

Name, city & interests woven throughout naturally

Last reply referenced and celebrated

Puzzle with a real consequence if answered correctly

Mail

To: Amelia, Birmingham

Dear Amelia,

We found the second entrance — the one you predicted would be near water. You were right. Atlas had to wade through to his waist to get to the door, which, it turns out, is older than any door should be.

Penny is keeping your last drawing on the wall of the camp. She says the shape in the bottom right corner might be significant. We need you to look again and tell us what you think it is.

— The Wandering Five
