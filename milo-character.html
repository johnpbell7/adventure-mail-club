Milo's story

The boy who builds things that shouldn't work.

Milo's bedroom looks like a workshop exploded. Gears, wires, crystals, and half-finished gadgets cover every surface. He once built a torch that only works underground and a compass that points to lost things.

He's the group's inventor. When the path is blocked, Milo builds a way through. His robot companion, Bolt, sits on his shoulder and beeps when danger is near.

His quest is to rebuild the Skyward Machine — an ancient device scattered across the world in pieces. Each chapter uncovers a new component, but assembling it requires solving mechanical puzzles that get harder each time.
