Why we built this.

Because the best stories are the ones you are part of.

Our mission

Stories that do something.

“We built Adventure Mail Club because we wanted to give children a story that actually needed them in it.”

We are a small team of writers, designers and childhood development enthusiasts. We started with one simple question: what if a story arrived by post, and it genuinely changed based on what the child wrote back?

Not just as a fun feature. As the actual engine of the storytelling. Where a child's reply one month literally determines a door that opens, a character that appears, a location that gets discovered.

We think reading should feel like adventure. And adventure should feel like it belongs to the child who is reading it.
