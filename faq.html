Common questions.

Everything you need to know before you start.

Product
What ages is Adventure Mail Club for?
Ages 5 to 10. We adjust the reading level, vocabulary and puzzle difficulty based on your child's age when you subscribe.
How personalised are the letters really?
Very. Your child's name, city, interests and previous replies are woven into the narrative structure — not just dropped in at the start. If they mention a dog in their reply, the dog reappears in the next letter. If they draw something, it gets referenced.
Do they have to write back?
No — but the experience is significantly richer when they do. Replies are used in the next letter. The story takes slightly different paths depending on answers. Children who write back tend to be more engaged with each arrival.
Is there a digital component?
Yes. Each pack includes a QR code that links to a digital adventure portal. Children can scan it to unlock artefact descriptions, see their world map, make mission choices, and track their inventory and travel log.
Delivery & shipping
When does the first letter arrive?
Within 5 working days of subscribing. Subsequent letters arrive in the first week of each month.
Do you ship outside the UK?
Currently UK only for postal deliveries. International digital access is in development. Join the waitlist.
Billing & cancellation
Can I cancel at any time?
Yes. Cancel before your next billing date from your account settings. No penalties, no questions.
Can I pause instead of cancelling?
Yes. You can pause for up to 3 months from your account settings. The story picks up where it left off when you resume.
What if I have more than one child?
Multi-child discounts apply from the second subscription. Each child gets their own fully personalised, independent story and portal account. Contact us for pricing.
