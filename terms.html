The subscription

Your subscription entitles you to receive one personalised adventure letter pack per month for the duration of your subscription. Subscriptions begin on the date of your first payment and renew automatically until cancelled.

Payment and billing

Payments are taken monthly (or annually for annual plans) via Stripe. Your card will be charged on the same date each month. Prices are shown inclusive of UK VAT where applicable. We reserve the right to change prices with 30 days' notice.

Cancellation

You may cancel at any time from your account dashboard or by contacting us. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you will continue to receive letters until then. We do not offer refunds for partial months.

Delivery

We aim to dispatch letters within 5 working days of the start of each subscription period. Delivery is via Royal Mail First Class. We are not responsible for delays caused by Royal Mail. If a letter is lost in post, we will resend it free of charge.
